Perintah:

  • Hitung determinan menggunakan rumus ekspansi baris: $\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+k}a_{ik}M_{ik}$
  • Gunakan rumus matriks adjoin untuk menghitung invers: $(\text{adj } A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j}M_{ji}$
  • Hasilnya ditulis dan dijelaskan dengan proses-prosesnya sesuai rumus di web statis GitHub.

Bagian A: Menghitung Determinan (Ekspansi Baris dengan Minor)

1. Matriks 2x2

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}$$

Ekspansi sepanjang baris pertama ($i = 1$):

  • $\det A = a_{11}(-1)^{1+1}M_{11} + a_{12}(-1)^{1+2}M_{12}$
  • $\det A = (-7)(1)(4) + (-5)(-1)(1)$
  • $\det A = -28 + 5 = \mathbf{-23}$

2. Matriks 3x3

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

Ekspansi pada baris ketiga ($i = 3$) karena memiliki elemen nol terbanyak untuk mempermudah perhitungan:

  • $\det A = a_{31}(-1)^{3+1}M_{31} + a_{32}(-1)^{3+2}M_{32} + a_{33}(-1)^{3+3}M_{33}$
  • $\det A = 0 \cdot M_{31} + 0 \cdot M_{32} + 1(-1)^{6} \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix}$
  • $\det A = 1(1)((0)(-2) - (2)(1))$
  • $\det A = 1(0 - 2) = \mathbf{-2}$

3. Matriks 4x4

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}$$

Melakukan ekspansi pada baris pertama ($i = 1$):

$$\det A = a_{11}(-1)^{1+1}M_{11} + a_{12}(-1)^{1+2}M_{12} + a_{13}(-1)^{1+3}M_{13} + a_{14}(-1)^{1+4}M_{14}$$

Langkah pencarian masing-masing Minor ($M_{ij}$):

  • Mencari $M_{11}$:
    $M_{11} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} = 1(9 - 1) - 1(-3 - 1) + 1(1 - (-3)) = 8 + 4 + 4 = 16$
  • Mencari $M_{12}$:
    $M_{12} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} = -3(9 - 1) - 1(-3 - 1) + 1(1 - (-3)) = -24 + 4 + 4 = -16$
  • Mencari $M_{13}$:
    $M_{13} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} = -3(-3 - 1) - 1(-3 - 1) + 1(1 - 1) = 12 + 4 + 0 = 16$
  • Mencari $M_{14}$:
    $M_{14} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = -3(1 - (-3)) - 1(1 - (-3)) + 1(1 - 1) = -12 - 4 + 0 = -16$

Substitusi ke rumus determinan:

  • $\det A = 1(1)(16) + (-3)(-1)(-16) + 1(1)(16) + 1(-1)(-16)$
  • $\det A = 16 - 48 + 16 + 16 = \mathbf{0}$

Bagian B: Menghitung Invers Matriks dengan Rumus Adjoin

Rumus matriks invers: $A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \text{adj } A$
Dimana elemen adjoin dihitung dengan transposisi minor: $(\text{adj } A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j}M_{ji}$

4. Invers Matriks 2x2

Berdasarkan perhitungan soal No. 1, $\det A = -23$.

  • $(\text{adj } A)_{11} = (-1)^{1+1}M_{11} = (1)(4) = 4$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{12} = (-1)^{1+2}M_{21} = (-1)(-5) = 5$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{21} = (-1)^{2+1}M_{12} = (-1)(1) = -1$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{22} = (-1)^{2+2}M_{22} = (1)(-7) = -7$
$$\text{adj } A = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix}$$

Sehingga Inversnya:

$$A^{-1} = -\frac{1}{23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} = \mathbf{\begin{bmatrix} -4/23 & -5/23 \\ 1/23 & 7/23 \end{bmatrix}}$$

5. Invers Matriks 3x3

Berdasarkan perhitungan soal No. 2, $\det A = -2$. Menghitung elemen adjoin secara langsung:

  • $(\text{adj } A)_{11} = (-1)^{2}M_{11} = (1)(0 - 2) = -2$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{12} = (-1)^{3}M_{21} = (-1)(2 - 0) = -2$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{13} = (-1)^{4}M_{31} = (1)(-2 - 6) = -8$

  • $(\text{adj } A)_{21} = (-1)^{3}M_{12} = (-1)(1 - 0) = -1$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{22} = (-1)^{4}M_{22} = (1)(0 - 0) = 0$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{23} = (-1)^{5}M_{32} = (-1)(0 - (-3)) = -3$

  • $(\text{adj } A)_{31} = (-1)^{4}M_{13} = (1)(0 - 0) = 0$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{32} = (-1)^{5}M_{23} = (-1)(0 - 0) = 0$
  • $(\text{adj } A)_{33} = (-1)^{6}M_{33} = (1)(0 - 2) = -2$
$$\text{adj } A = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8 \\ -1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix}$$

Sehingga Inversnya:

$$A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-2} \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8 \\ -1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} = \mathbf{\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4 \\ 1/2 & 0 & 3/2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}}$$

6. Invers Matriks 4x4

Berdasarkan perhitungan ekspansi baris di soal No. 3, kita telah mendapatkan bahwa nilai determinan matriks ini adalah $\det A = 0$.

Sesuai rumus $A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \text{adj } A$, pembagian dengan nilai nol tidak terdefinisi. Matriks dengan determinan nol adalah matriks singular.

Kesimpulan: Matriks pada soal nomor 6 tidak memiliki invers.
